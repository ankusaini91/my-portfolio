A Login Form

A login form is a user interface element that allows users to enter their credentials (usually a username and password) to gain access to a system or application.

A Portfolio

A portfolio is a curated collection of documents, projects, or other materials that showcase an individual's skills, accomplishments, and professional experience. Portfolios are often used in creative fields like design, photography, and writing, but they can be beneficial in nearly any profession.Portfolios can be digital or physical, depending on the industry and purpose. Digital portfolios might be presented as personal websites, PDFs, or through platforms specifically designed for showcasing work.

Photo Gallery

A photo gallery is a webpage or a section of a website designed to display a collection of images. It provides an organized and visually appealing way to showcase photos, allowing users to browse through them easily.
